Background

Through surveying small business proprietors (n=60), we came to the conclusion that during the COVID-19 Restriction had made most of their traditional operation redudant and unproductive.
Factors that were greatly affected were; Customer Relationships, Brick and mortar operations and Optimization. We also empathized knowing that most small business are operated to provide for its owner and employees, and without it running efficiently, would have dentrimental affects.


Solution

"Social-Media for Businesses" where businesses are able to reach out to other service providing business that specializes in the digital transformation field. The platform shows the profile of those service providing business, and how it has digitize other small businesses. Like AWS' WhitePapers, the products that was used can also be used for other similar businesses looking to digitize themselves(DIY)
